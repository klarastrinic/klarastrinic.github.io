15. Babushka Boi by A$AP Rocky

“Babushka” is Russian for “grandmother” or “elderly woman,”
and it’s also a type of Russian headscarf tied under the chin,
traditionally worn by women from Eastern Europe.
In November 2018, A$AP Rocky appeared at the LACMA
Art+Film Gala in Los Angeles, wearing a $495 Gucci babushka.
This song is pure masterpiece
